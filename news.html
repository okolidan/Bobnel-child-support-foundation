Bobnel foundation seeks support for world-class centre

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation celebrated its 20th anniversary with a fundraiser to launch a world-class orphanage for vulnerable and physically challenged children. May 25, 2019

BOBNEL to Raise N1bn for Orphanage

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation plans to raise ₦1 billion in August to build a home for 50 physically challenged and less-privileged children, on land donated anonymously. Executive Director Mrs. Cornelia Udoenoh is appealing for public support to make the project a reality, aligning with the foundation’s 20-year mission to support children’s physical, emotional, and educational needs.
