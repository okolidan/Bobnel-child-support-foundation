Our Mission

The BOBNEL Child Support Foundation (BOBNEL CSF) is a non-governmental, humanitarian organization dedicated to improving the lives of challenged and less privileged children across Nigeria. Since its establishment, the foundation has remained committed to addressing the fundamental rights, needs, and development of children living in difficult conditions, particularly those with physical and intellectual challenges. The primary objective of BOBNEL CSF is to cater to the rights and well-being of challenged children, ensuring they have equal opportunities in education and vocational training, access to medical care, and provision of basic necessities such as food, clothing, and shelter.

Core Activities and Achievements

Annual Free Christmas Party

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation organizes a yearly Free Christmas Party to bring joy, excitement, and a festive spirit to challenged and less privileged children. This event provides an opportunity for the children to celebrate, receive gifts, enjoy meals, and participate in fun activities, making the holiday season memorable and inclusive for all.

Provision of Medical Care

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation provides vital medical support through annual free eye screenings and other health-related services. This initiative ensures that children in need receive early diagnosis, treatment, and ongoing care to support their overall health and well-being.

Financial Support for Education

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation offers financial assistance for school fees to less privileged children, helping to eliminate barriers to education. This support ensures that every child has the opportunity to attend school and build a brighter future.

Provision of Clothes, Drugs, and Food

BOBNEL Child Support Foundation supplies essential clothes, medications, and both cooked and uncooked food to children in need, ensuring their basic health, nutrition, and comfort are well cared for.

Share-A-Smile Project

The Share-A-Smile Project by BOBNEL Child Support Foundation focuses on improving the lives of challenged and less privileged children by protecting them from malnutrition, homelessness, illnesses, illiteracy, and discomfort, ultimately bringing joy and hope to their lives.
